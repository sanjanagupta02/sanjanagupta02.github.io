Observation Planner

Proposal deadlines, elevation and visibility checks for radio (VLA, GMRT, MeerKAT, ASKAP) and X-ray facilities (Chandra, Swift, NuSTAR, XMM-Newton), and coordinate/time converters. Look up any supernova from the catalog by name to fill coordinates automatically.
