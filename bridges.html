Bridges

Science communication and outreach. Astronomy belongs to everyone, and I want it to reach as many people as possible.

Talking about astronomy outside the lab or the classroom is something I've always found myself drawn to. If you'd like to work on something together, get in touch.

Outreach

Member of Dark Skies Bright Kids at the Department of Astronomy, University of Virginia, an outreach program bringing astronomy to elementary and middle school kids through hands-on activities and stargazing events.

Leadership

Teaching
