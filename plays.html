Theatre I have watched.

Play Date Venue Language About
Raktakarabi 13 May 2023 Girish Mancha, Kolkata Bangla By Tagore; a modern, post-COVID adaptation. Directed by Kaushik Chattopadhyay.
Apurboshoti 14 May 2023 Academy of Fine Arts, Kolkata Bangla By Sukumari Dutta; the first play in Bangla written by a woman, and somewhat autobiographical.
Haraner Natjamai May 2023 Academy of Fine Arts, Kolkata Bangla Set during the Tebhaga movement. Bhuban Mondal leads an effort to unite farmers against the local zamindar and police. Das, who sacrifices his life in the struggle, is Haran's son; his family continues to support Mondal. Mondal evades capture by disguising himself as Haran's natjamai (grandson-in-law), an idea credited to Moyna's mother. The real natjamai, Jagamohan, is also central: he defies his moneylender father and brings his wife Moyna home despite an unpaid dowry. Elements of jatra throughout; police brutality and the corruption of moneylenders shown unflinchingly.
Binodini Opera May 2023 Madhusudan Mancha, Kolkata Bangla A musical on the life of Noti Binodini. The ending draws on Brechtian Epic Theatre. Directed by Padmanabha Dasgupta; Sudipta Chakraborty played Binodini.
