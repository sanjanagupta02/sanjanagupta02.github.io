হারানের নাতজামাই · Haraner Natjamai 17 May 2023 Academy of Fine Arts Set during the Tebhaga movement. Bhuban Mondal leads an effort to unite farmers against the local zamindar and police. Das, who sacrifices his life in the struggle, is Haran's son; his family continues to support Mondal. Mondal evades capture by disguising himself as Haran's natjamai (grandson-in-law), an idea credited to Moyna's mother. The real natjamai, Jagamohan, is also central: he defies his moneylender father and brings his wife Moyna home despite an unpaid dowry. Elements of jatra throughout; police brutality and the corruption of moneylenders shown unflinchingly.
